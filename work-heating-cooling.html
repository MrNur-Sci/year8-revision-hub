1. Key Ideas

Work done

Work is done when a force moves an object through a distance.

Heating

Heating transfers energy from hotter objects to cooler surroundings.

Insulation

Insulation reduces unwanted energy transfer.

3. Heating and Cooling Transfers

Conduction

Particles vibrate and pass energy along a solid.

Convection

Warm fluid rises and cool fluid sinks.

Radiation

Infrared radiation can travel through empty space.
