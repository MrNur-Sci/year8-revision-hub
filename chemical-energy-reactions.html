1. Exothermic and Endothermic Reactions

Exothermic reactions transfer energy to the surroundings, so temperature rises. Endothermic reactions take in energy from the surroundings, so temperature falls.

3. Combustion Practical: Complete and Incomplete Combustion

Combustion needs fuel, oxygen and heat. Complete combustion makes carbon dioxide and water. Incomplete combustion happens when oxygen is limited and can produce carbon monoxide and soot.
